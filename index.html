Email Signatures

Five mark styles across the whole team. Pick a tab, then Copy signature to paste straight in or Download .html. Every one is a self-contained dark card (white marks, yellow honeycomb, on a locked dark ground) so it renders identically in Outlook, Gmail, Apple Mail and on mobile, light or dark, no client can strip or invert it.
