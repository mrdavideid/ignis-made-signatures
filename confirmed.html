Email Signatures

One layout, four formats, the whole team. Universal is the safe pick: every mark is yellow, the one colour a dark-mode inbox can never flip, so it stays sharp in Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail and on mobile, on a light or dark background. Light and Dark are fixed-ground cards. Original is the classic black look with no card, for a light inbox. Pick a tab, then Copy signature or Download .html.
