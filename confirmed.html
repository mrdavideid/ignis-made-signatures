Email Signatures

The same white rounded card, built five ways, so you can paste all five and see which holds up in Gmail (light and dark) and Outlook. Tabs 1 and 2 are real HTML (crisp, selectable, tappable, but Gmail dark recolours the text). Tabs 3, 4, 5 are flat images, the text stays black and every element is a real tappable link, plain, bordered, and with a soft shadow. Use the Light / Dark toggle to preview, then Copy or Download.
