Email Signature System

Rami Shakour, Co-Founder. Ten signature directions, one brand. The colour discipline: #FFC72C is black-native, so on white it only ever appears as a solid shape (a rule, the honeycomb, a frame). On black it becomes type. The bare MADE wordmark leads; the honeycomb is a deliberate guest.

Install: click Copy signature under any design, then paste into your mail client signature editor (Gmail Settings · Outlook Signatures · Apple Mail Preferences).
